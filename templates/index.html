My Poem

Nobody Loves the Kryptonite Man Nobody loves the Kryptonite Man Everybody loves the guy in the tights Who flies high in sky? And can bend steel with his hands But nobody loves the Kryptonite man Why? Is it because his poisonous touch Is it because he talks too much Telling of his plans So the man can figure a way To spoil them Is it because of the way he dresses? It is almost obscene Wearing the color lime green Out season, for no reason Just because he can So the Man from Krypton Can do no wrong But the kryptonite man is a leper That no one wants around Up is surely down When you are the Kryptonite Man Soundtrack: “Lonesome Road- Madeline Peyroux”
